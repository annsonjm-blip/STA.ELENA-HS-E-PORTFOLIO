Welcome!

Welcome to our Internship E-Portfolio, a digital compilation of our journey as student teachers under the Practice Teaching/Internship (EDUC 024) program from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Sta. Mesa, Manila. This website serves as a reflection of our experiences, growth, dedication, and learning throughout our internship in the field of education. It highlights the meaningful moments, challenges, and accomplishments we encountered as we prepared ourselves to become future educators.

Inside this portfolio, you will find our daily journals, documentations, pictures, and performance tasks that showcase our teaching experiences and professional development. It also contains important supporting documents such as our Daily Time Records (DTR), certificates, lesson plans, final demonstration teaching materials, presentations, evaluations, memorandum of agreement, endorsement letters, and other requirements related to our internship journey.

In addition, this e-portfolio introduces our cooperating school and presents the members of our group of student teachers who worked together throughout this meaningful experience. Through this platform, we aim to demonstrate not only our academic requirements but also our passion, commitment, and readiness to serve in the teaching profession.

We hope this portfolio provides a clear glimpse of our growth and experiences as aspiring educators, and serves as a testament to the knowledge, skills, and values we have developed during our internship journey.
